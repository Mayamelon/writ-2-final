Cyan Hub

The core of any good product, the Cyan Hub is required to connect to peripherals of your choice to start collecting data. It contains an onboard radio to automatically transmit data to our servers. Also included are a GPS, microphones, and cameras to begin collecting data and five general purpose input outputs (GPIOs) to connect to your product.

Starting at $9.99, bulk pricing available


Cyan Camera

The Cyan Camera peripheral will easily integrate with a Cyan Hub. State of the art tracking technology gives the camera a near 360 degree field of view. The Cyan Camera has unparalleled quality and resolution which can help you build a profile of your customers.

Starting at $19.99, bulk pricing available


Cyan Facial Recognizer

The Cyan Facial Recognizer interfaces directly with the Cyan Camera and can quickly discern who is nearby. With memory that can support up to 20 people, the Cyan Facial Recognizer is crucial for building detailed profiles for products used by multiple people.

Starting at $19.99, bulk pricing available


Cyan Biometrics

Our latest product, Cyan Biometrics has the most sophisticated sensor package yet. With infrared heart rate and temperature sensors, you can tell when your customers are actively engaging with your products. State of the art, innovative sensor technology allows you to maintain data gathering even when customers are 10 feet away.

Starting at $24.99, bulk pricing available
