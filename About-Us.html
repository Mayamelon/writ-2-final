The Vision

When Cyan Customer-Circuits was founded in 2028, we had a vision to change the world of marketing and advertisment forever. Now, after eight years, our dream has come true. Our goal is to help business collect data from their customers most effectively so that they can make their business as profitable as possible. To this end, our business model involves selling our products and services at a loss to businesses. We make this profit back by selling the data we collect to third party advertisment partners and to US government contracts and partnerships. As we are located right in the heart of Silicon Valley, we are the best option for combining the high-tech computer chip industry with the algorithm expertise of the software industry.


History

  • 2028 - Cyan Customer-Circuits founded in Santa Cruz, California
  • 2030 - First iteration product line developed
  • 2032 - United States NSA grant secured, moved to new headquarters in Palo Alto, California
  • 2032 - First early adopters and increased funding secured
  • 2034 - Mass production, deployment, and sales; 60% marketshare in advertisment industry reached
